Understanding Liquidation HeatMaps

The Liquidation HeatMap displays potential liquidation levels based on order book depth, leverage parameters, and current funding rates. Bright colors (yellow/red) represent large clusters of leverage orders (potential liquidation pools) that act as magnets for price action. Market makers and large institutional players often drive the price toward these areas to trigger cascade liquidations and capture liquidity.
